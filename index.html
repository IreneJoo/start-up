Dailynew

Why we develop DailyNew?

DailyNew is a start-up by several master and undergraduate students in Shenzhen,China. Based on the university environment and need of transportation of different objects, DailyNew is trying to launch an application in order to provide the students with an easy way to access the things they need on campus.

Apply for DailyNew

Product Delivery

DailyNew has several student deliverymen and deliverywomen live in different buildings on campus. Thus they can delivery the products and takeout things from the product containers and different gates directly to students' dormitory.

Warehousing on campus

DailyNew is trying to fix the WareHousing on campus project in order to meet the need of the exchanging second-hand and other types of products.

With special containers on campus connected to the application, students can get the products they ordered and put their products into the containers with the verification of their personal information.
